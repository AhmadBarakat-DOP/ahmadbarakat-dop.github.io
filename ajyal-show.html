Ajyal Show

Join us on a captivating journey into the heart of creativity and cinematic expression with The Ajyal Show. This bilingual podcast, meticulously crafted by DFI interns and Ajyal Film Festival alumni, bridges cultures and connects aspiring creatives, storytellers, and cinema enthusiasts with the insights and experiences of local and regional experts.

As the Director and Director of Photography across 32 episodes and two seasons, I helped shape a visual and narrative tone that spotlights young voices and regional talent. Each episode blends behind-the-scenes filmmaking with youth-driven storytelling in an elevated, cinematic podcast format.

The Ajyal Show exemplifies the dynamic energy and innovative spirit of Doha’s creative community. Through our fascinating, engaging, and insightful discussions, we uncover the art of storytelling, explore cinema’s power to reflect and reshape society, and inspire new generations of filmmakers.
