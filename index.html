About

Ahmad Barakat is a filmmaker based in Doha, Qatar. A 2024 graduate of Northwestern University in Qatar, Ahmad has shot two seasons of the Ajyal Show podcast (32 episodes total) and is set to shoot three DFI-funded short films by September 2025. His cinematography credits include Lehrein (2023), Yom El Jum’a (2024), and Ana Batal (2025). Ahmad has also served as 1st AC on over 10 productions across QFF, DFI, and Studio 20Q films. His work blends a hands-on visual style with youth-focused storytelling.
